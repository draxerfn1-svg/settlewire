Kalshi Pro Trading Terminal Targets High-Volume Prediction Market Users

Published JUL 13, 2026 · 12:51 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

Kalshi is launching a professional trading terminal designed for its most active prediction market traders, according to a report by qz.com.

Kalshi, the CFTC-regulated prediction market exchange, is rolling out a dedicated pro trading terminal aimed at its heaviest-volume users, according to qz.com. The move signals the platform's push to attract sophisticated traders who require more advanced tooling than the standard consumer interface currently offers.

The terminal represents a significant product expansion for Kalshi, which has positioned itself as the leading regulated event-contracts venue in the United States. Professional-grade interfaces — featuring faster order execution, deeper market data, and customizable layouts — are standard in equities and crypto exchanges but have been largely absent from the prediction markets space.

Kalshi has seen rapid growth following court victories that affirmed its right to offer political and economic event contracts under CFTC oversight. Building infrastructure for pro traders could accelerate liquidity on the platform, a key metric that separates nascent prediction markets from mature financial exchanges.

Why this matters

A pro terminal lowers friction for market makers and arbitrageurs, which should tighten spreads and improve price accuracy across Kalshi's event contracts — directly benefiting retail traders as a second-order effect. For competitors like Polymarket, this raises the bar on product sophistication in the regulated U.S. market. Regulators watching event-contract venues will also note that institutional-grade tooling signals Kalshi's intent to court professional capital, potentially inviting fresh scrutiny of position limits and reporting requirements.
