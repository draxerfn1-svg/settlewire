Sinner Heavy Kalshi Favorite Over Djokovic at Wimbledon

Published JUL 08, 2026 · 15:29 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

Jannik Sinner is listed as a heavy favorite against Novak Djokovic on Kalshi's Wimbledon prediction market, according to Sports Illustrated's coverage of the event contract.

Kalshi's Wimbledon event contracts have priced Jannik Sinner as a decisive favorite over Novak Djokovic, with Sports Illustrated flagging the matchup as a marquee liquidity event on the regulated U.S. prediction market platform. The specific contract odds were highlighted as representing a significant implied probability gap between the two players heading into the match.

Kalshi, which won the legal right to list sports event contracts after its 2023 CFTC court victory, has expanded its sports market offerings to include major tennis tournaments including Wimbledon. The Sinner-Djokovic matchup represents one of the higher-profile individual match contracts the platform has listed, drawing mainstream sports media attention from outlets like Sports Illustrated.

The coverage signals growing crossover between traditional sports media and regulated prediction-market platforms. As Kalshi continues to build sports contract volume, individual match markets featuring top-ranked players like world No. 1 Sinner against an all-time great in Djokovic serve as flagship products for demonstrating retail trader engagement on a CFTC-regulated venue.

Why this matters

Mainstream sports media covering Kalshi match contracts — rather than just sportsbooks — marks a legitimacy milestone for regulated event-contract platforms that traders should watch as a volume catalyst. If Sinner-Djokovic draws significant open interest, it validates Kalshi's tennis vertical and could accelerate similar contract listings. Prediction-market operators competing for sports liquidity will monitor whether CFTC-regulated venues can capture mindshare from offshore sportsbooks through this kind of media exposure.
