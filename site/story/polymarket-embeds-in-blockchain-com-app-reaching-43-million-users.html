Polymarket Embeds in Blockchain.com App, Reaching 43 Million Users

Published JUL 14, 2026 · 11:00 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

Blockchain.com has partnered with Polymarket to integrate prediction-market event contracts directly into its brokerage app, giving Polymarket access to the platform's 43 million registered users.

Blockchain.com, one of the oldest crypto wallet and brokerage platforms with 43 million registered users, announced the Polymarket integration via PR Newswire. The deal embeds Polymarket's event-contract interface natively inside the Blockchain.com app, allowing existing users to trade prediction markets without leaving the platform or creating a separate Polymarket account.

The partnership is a distribution play for Polymarket, which has surged in visibility since the 2024 U.S. election cycle but has largely depended on direct web traffic and crypto-native audiences. Blockchain.com's brokerage user base — which spans retail investors across multiple countries — represents a materially different and broader demographic than Polymarket's current core.

According to Finance Magnates, Blockchain.com is explicitly framing the move as a response to 'event-trading demand' within its existing user base. The integration positions prediction markets alongside conventional crypto trading products, signaling that major infrastructure players now view event contracts as a standard retail asset class rather than a niche offering.

Why this matters

Embedding Polymarket inside a 43-million-user platform is the largest known distribution expansion for any prediction market to date, and volume and liquidity metrics on Polymarket markets could jump materially in coming months. For CFTC regulation watchers, mainstream brokerage distribution of event contracts raises fresh questions about whether existing event-contract exemptions can hold as user scale grows. Rival platforms like Kalshi and ForecastEx will face pressure to strike similar brokerage distribution deals or risk ceding the retail acquisition race.
