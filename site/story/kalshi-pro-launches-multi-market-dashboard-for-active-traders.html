Kalshi Pro Launches Multi-Market Dashboard for Active Traders

Published JUL 13, 2026 · 12:19 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

Kalshi has officially launched Kalshi Pro, a new platform tier designed for high-volume traders who simultaneously manage positions across multiple event-contract markets.

Kalshi Pro went live as a dedicated interface built for traders operating at scale across the prediction-market platform's growing catalog of event contracts, according to Bitcoin Foundation's coverage of the launch. The product targets users who find the standard Kalshi interface insufficient for tracking multiple simultaneous positions, offering an upgraded workflow designed around speed and breadth of market access.

The launch represents Kalshi's clearest move yet toward segmenting its user base between casual participants and professional-grade traders — a distinction common in traditional financial platforms but relatively novel in the regulated event-contract space. Kalshi is the only CFTC-designated contract market focused primarily on event contracts, giving it a unique regulatory footing that lends credibility to building out a 'pro' tier.

Specific pricing, feature details, and eligibility criteria for Kalshi Pro were not fully detailed in available coverage, but the product's existence signals Kalshi's intent to court institutional-adjacent users who demand more sophisticated tooling than a retail-oriented interface provides.

Why this matters

A professional trading tier at Kalshi could meaningfully deepen liquidity in its event markets by attracting sophisticated market-makers who currently find the platform's tools insufficient — a second-order boost to price accuracy that prediction-market observers should watch closely. For competing platforms like Polymarket, it raises the stakes on their own UX investments. Regulators may also scrutinize whether 'pro' access tiers create disparate information or execution advantages that require additional oversight under CFTC event-contract rules.
