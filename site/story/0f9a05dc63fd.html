CFTC sues New York, unveils framework to govern event contracts

Published APR 25, 2026 · 07:00 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

The CFTC filed suit against New York state while simultaneously proposing a new regulatory structure for sports-related event contracts.

The CFTC filed suit against New York state while simultaneously proposing a new regulatory structure for sports-related event contracts.

Why this matters

A federal win against New York would remove a key legal barrier for licensed prediction market operators seeking to expand sports contracts into major U.S. markets. Traders should watch whether the proposed framework creates standardized contract rules that lower compliance costs or instead impose new restrictions that narrow tradeable event categories.
