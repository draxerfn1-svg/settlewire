Kalshi market favors England over Argentina in knockout clash

Published JUL 13, 2026 · 17:15 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

A Kalshi prediction market on the England vs. Argentina match is pricing England as the favorite to advance, according to Sports Illustrated.

Sports Illustrated highlighted a Kalshi event contract tracking which team advances from the England vs. Argentina matchup, with market sentiment tilting toward England. Kalshi, the CFTC-regulated prediction market exchange, has expanded aggressively into sports event contracts since winning its legal battle to offer such products in the United States.

The coverage underscores how mainstream sports media is increasingly treating prediction-market pricing as a legitimate signal of expected outcomes, similar to how Vegas odds have long been cited. Kalshi's sports contracts allow U.S. users to trade on binary outcomes — team A advances or team B advances — creating real-money crowd forecasts.

The Sports Illustrated mention reflects a broader media trend of embedding Kalshi and Polymarket probabilities directly into sports previews and game-day coverage, giving the platforms earned media exposure and driving user acquisition without paid advertising.

Why this matters

Sports Illustrated's editorial decision to feature Kalshi market odds signals that regulated prediction markets are reaching mainstream credibility as a forecasting tool alongside traditional sportsbooks. For traders, growing media attention can create short-term liquidity spikes and sharper pricing as casual participants enter high-profile event contracts. Regulators watching CFTC-approved sports contracts will note this as evidence of real public engagement with the product category.
