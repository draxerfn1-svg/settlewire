Kalshi Home Run Derby market opens with Schwarber as frontrunner

Published JUL 13, 2026 · 18:30 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

Kalshi has launched a Home Run Derby prediction market listing Kyle Schwarber as the odds-on favorite to win the 2025 MLB All-Star event.

According to Sports Illustrated, Kalshi's Home Run Derby event contract places Kyle Schwarber at the top of the field, making him the market's implied favorite heading into the MLB All-Star weekend competition. The platform's contract allows traders to take positions on which slugger will claim the Derby crown.

Kalshi has increasingly expanded into sports event contracts following its CFTC legal victory that cleared the way for regulated prediction markets on sporting outcomes in the United States. The Home Run Derby market is one of several baseball-related contracts the platform has introduced for the 2025 MLB season.

Sports Illustrated highlighted the market as a novel way for fans to engage with the All-Star festivities, noting Schwarber's well-documented power numbers as the basis for his market-leading position. Other competitors in the Derby field are available to trade at longer implied odds on the Kalshi platform.

Why this matters

Kalshi's continued rollout of sports event contracts tests how deep retail appetite runs for regulated, non-sportsbook prediction markets on live sporting events — a key data point for CFTC watchers monitoring the post-ruling landscape. If volume on niche events like the Home Run Derby proves meaningful, it strengthens the commercial case for competitors like Polymarket to seek similar U.S. regulatory clearance. Traders should watch whether Schwarber's favorite status holds or shifts as rosters are confirmed, since liquidity and line movement on early sports markets signal platform health.
