Polymarket 'ROCKY' referral code extended through MLB All-Star Game

Published JUL 13, 2026 · 01:03 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

Polymarket has extended its $50 referral promotion using code 'ROCKY' through the MLB All-Star Game, according to Rocky Top Insider.

Rocky Top Insider reports that Polymarket's $50 referral code 'ROCKY' has been extended to run through the MLB All-Star Game, giving new users an incentive window tied to one of baseball's marquee mid-season events. The All-Star Game is scheduled for mid-July, meaning the promotion gives prospective sign-ups a clear, sports-calendar-anchored deadline to join the platform.

Referral campaigns have become a standard customer-acquisition tool for prediction market platforms competing for retail traders. Polymarket, the largest decentralized prediction market by volume, has previously used sports and political milestones as natural hooks to time promotional pushes, aligning sign-up incentives with moments of elevated public interest in forecasting outcomes.

The $50 incentive is notable in the context of Polymarket's broader U.S. expansion efforts following regulatory scrutiny. Tying promotions to mainstream American sports events like the MLB All-Star Game signals a deliberate move to grow the platform's user base beyond its crypto-native core audience and toward general sports and news followers.

Why this matters

Referral-driven user growth directly inflates liquidity on active markets, which tightens spreads and improves price discovery — a metric traders watch closely. Operators competing with Kalshi and ForecastEx should note that milestone-anchored promotions are an emerging playbook for retail acquisition. Regulation watchers may also flag whether such incentive structures invite additional CFTC scrutiny of Polymarket's U.S. onboarding practices.
