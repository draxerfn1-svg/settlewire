Polymarket Signs Crypto Partnership Ahead of World Cup Semifinals

Published JUL 14, 2026 · 10:00 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

Polymarket announced a partnership with an unnamed crypto firm timed to the FIFA World Cup semifinals, while simultaneously running a $50 promotional offer for new users via code "OREGON".

Polymarket is leveraging the high-traffic World Cup semifinal window to expand its brand footprint, partnering with a crypto firm according to Bloomberg.com, though specific financial terms of the deal were not disclosed in available coverage. The timing signals an intentional push to capture sports-driven prediction-market volume during one of the calendar's most-watched events.

Separately, OregonLive.com reported that Polymarket updated its $50 promo code "OREGON" to cover both World Cup semifinal markets and MLB All-Star Game contracts. Promotional codes tied to specific sporting events are a relatively new customer-acquisition tactic for the platform, suggesting Polymarket is competing aggressively for bettors already familiar with sports wagering.

The dual announcements — a crypto-sector partnership and a direct-to-consumer promo code — reflect Polymarket's broader strategy of growing liquidity and user base simultaneously. Prediction-market platforms depend heavily on market depth, and major sporting events historically produce sharp spikes in contract volume that can attract longer-term active traders.

Why this matters

A crypto-firm partnership could bring institutional liquidity or on-ramp infrastructure to Polymarket at a moment when sports-event contracts are its highest-visibility product. Traders should watch whether the partnership translates into tighter spreads or new contract categories in the weeks following the tournament. Regulators tracking event-contract marketing — particularly the Oregon-branded promo — may scrutinize whether such geo-coded promotions imply jurisdictional targeting.
