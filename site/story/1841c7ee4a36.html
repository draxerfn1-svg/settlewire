Why this matters

Traders face platform-level risk if exchanges operating under CFTC approval suddenly encounter state-level enforcement actions that could freeze activity or force delisting of contracts. Operators and investors must now price in jurisdictional fragmentation, which could splinter liquidity and raise compliance costs significantly.
