Third Circuit backs Kalshi, upholds block on congressional bet ban

Published APR 09, 2026 · 07:00 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

A federal appeals court affirmed a preliminary injunction allowing Kalshi to continue offering contracts on congressional election outcomes while litigation proceeds.

A federal appeals court affirmed a preliminary injunction allowing Kalshi to continue offering contracts on congressional election outcomes while litigation proceeds.

Why this matters

The ruling strengthens Kalshi's legal standing and signals that courts are skeptical of CFTC authority to categorically ban political event contracts, which could embolden other platforms to list similar products. Traders pricing regulatory risk on competing platforms should treat this as a meaningful reduction in the probability of a near-term federal crackdown on election markets.
