CFTC moves forward with formal prediction market rules

Published MAR 20, 2026 · 17:03 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

The CFTC has taken concrete steps toward establishing a dedicated regulatory framework governing prediction market event contracts in the United States.

The CFTC has taken concrete steps toward establishing a dedicated regulatory framework governing prediction market event contracts in the United States.

Why this matters

A clearer ruleset could unlock institutional capital by reducing compliance uncertainty, directly boosting liquidity on platforms like Kalshi and Polymarket. Conversely, codified rules may impose operational costs or contract-approval burdens that disadvantage smaller or offshore operators.
