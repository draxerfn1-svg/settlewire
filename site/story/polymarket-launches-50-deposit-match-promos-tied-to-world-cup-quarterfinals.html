Polymarket launches $50 deposit-match promos tied to World Cup quarterfinals

Published JUL 10, 2026 · 12:00 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

Polymarket is running partner-specific $50 bonus promotions in July 2025, requiring a $20 deposit and unique promo codes, targeting prediction-market activity around 2026 World Cup quarterfinal matches and political events.

Polymarket distributed outlet-specific promo codes — including NYPMAX, SPORTSLINE, DIME, FOX, and OREGON, among others — each unlocking a $50 trade bonus upon a $20 deposit. The campaign, active around July 10, 2025, is timed to Spain vs. Belgium in the World Cup quarterfinals, according to coverage from the New York Post, SportsLine, and OregonLive.

FOX Sports noted its code also applies to Conor McGregor vs. Max Holloway alongside the soccer matches, signaling Polymarket is bundling combat sports and football into a single acquisition push. The multi-outlet rollout spans mainstream sports media, golf publications like Golfweek, and NBA team blogs such as Denver Stiffs, reflecting a broad affiliate strategy.

The promotion represents a notable retail user-acquisition effort by Polymarket, which has historically relied on crypto-native audiences. Tying bonuses to specific match events — rather than open-ended deposits — creates natural expiration pressure and concentrates liquidity in high-profile short-duration contracts.

Why this matters

A coordinated affiliate promo push signals Polymarket is actively competing for casual sports bettors, which could pressure Kalshi and ForecastEx to respond with their own incentive programs. Traders should watch whether the liquidity spike on World Cup contracts distorts short-term pricing on those markets. Regulators tracking retail marketing practices at CFTC-adjacent platforms will likely take note of the scale and sports-media reach of this campaign.
