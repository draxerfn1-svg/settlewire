CFTC launches first-ever formal rulemaking for prediction markets

Published MAR 14, 2026 · 07:00 UTC · SettleWire newsroom

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission has initiated a formal rulemaking process that would establish the first comprehensive federal regulatory framework specifically governing prediction markets.

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission has initiated a formal rulemaking process that would establish the first comprehensive federal regulatory framework specifically governing prediction markets.

Why this matters

Codified rules could legitimize event contracts as an asset class, attracting institutional capital and broader operator entry — but compliance costs may consolidate the market around well-funded incumbents. Traders should watch whether final rules constrain contract types or position limits, as either could directly reprice liquidity on existing platforms.
