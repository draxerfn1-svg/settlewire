About SettleWire

SettleWire is the intelligence layer for prediction markets. We monitor coverage from more than 90 news outlets, financial wires, and trade publications, cluster everything written about a single event into one story, and add the context that matters: what happened, who reported it first, and why it moves the needle for traders, operators, and regulation watchers.

How our stories work

Every story on this site is written in our own words by our editorial pipeline. We never republish source articles. Each story credits the outlet that reported it first, links to their original reporting, and lists every other outlet covering the same event. If you want the source's full account, it's always one click away.

Coverage scope

We cover prediction markets and event contracts exclusively: exchanges like Polymarket and Kalshi, CFTC and international regulation, deals and funding, market data, and the technology underneath. If it's not about this industry, it doesn't run here.
