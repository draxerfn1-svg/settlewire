Risk Disclosure

Prediction markets involve real financial risk. Positions in event contracts can lose their entire value. Prices reflect crowd sentiment and available liquidity, not guaranteed probabilities of outcomes.

This site is not advice

Nothing published on SettleWire is a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any contract on any platform. Odds and figures cited in stories are point-in-time snapshots that may be stale by the time you read them.

Regulatory status varies

The legal status of prediction-market platforms differs by country and, in the United States, by state — and it changes frequently. Access rules, product availability, and enforcement posture may shift with little notice. You are responsible for knowing what is lawful where you live.

Do your own research

Always verify claims with primary sources, understand a platform's settlement and custody rules before depositing funds, and never stake money you cannot afford to lose.
